How It Works

From a drama file and subtitle to a published YouTube video in minutes.

1

Upload Subtitle & Video

Upload your drama or movie file along with its SRT subtitle file through the web UI.

2

AI Analyzes Content

Google Gemini 2.5 Flash scans the full transcript and extracts the best expressions, idioms, and phrases for learning.

3

Video Clips Generated

FFmpeg extracts frame-accurate clips with dual-language subtitles and educational slides — automatically.

4

Auto-Upload to YouTube

Videos are scheduled and uploaded to your YouTube channel with AI-generated titles and descriptions.

Capabilities

Built for Production

A full-stack AI platform with modern architecture and real engineering depth.

🤖

Google Gemini 2.5 Flash

2M token context window processes entire episodes at once. 95.5% accuracy with advanced prompt engineering and structured JSON output.

🎥

Frame-Accurate Video Processing

FFmpeg pipeline with 0.1-second precision clip extraction, multi-codec support, and professional subtitle overlay rendering.

⚡

FastAPI Backend

Async microservices with 180ms average response time, Redis job queues, and optional PostgreSQL for job tracking.

📺

YouTube Auto-Upload

OAuth 2.0 integration with the YouTube Data API. Schedule uploads, generate metadata, and manage your channel — all automated.

🌐

Multi-Language Support

Configurable source and target languages. Dual-language subtitles with custom font rendering for CJK and Latin scripts.

🏗️

Clean Architecture

35K+ lines, 1,088 functions, 162 classes, 88.9% type hint coverage. Modular design with comprehensive test suite.
